Turning Data Into Insightful Stories

It all started with a college project. At first, I just wanted to finish my courses and meet deadlines. The assignment was straightforward: examine some data and offer findings.

But what began as a mundane task soon evolved into something far more fascinating—the pleasure of discovering stories concealed in seemingly random figures, the challenge of deciphering numbers, and the patterns in the data completely captivated me.

Professional Journey

From handling complex payroll operations to mastering data analysis, my journey has been driven by a passion for learning and continuous improvement.
With hands-on experience at Paychex, I’ve worked on enhancing data accuracy and streamlining processes, while diving deep into SQL, Python, and statistical analysis to unlock insights.
Every step has been about refining my skills, embracing new challenges, and applying data-driven solutions to real-world problems.

Continuous Growth

Now, I continue to build on that foundation—constantly learning, practicing new techniques, and sharpening my ability to translate raw data into meaningful impact.
Whether it's writing more efficient SQL queries, experimenting with new Python libraries, or exploring machine learning concepts, I’m committed to growing every day.
Looking forward to the next chapter!

Beyond Data

Outside the world of data, I like to unwind with a good badminton match, dive into great stories, and explore the best coffee spots and ceramics in town.
I believe real progress is driven by curiosity—whether it’s about numbers, people, or the little details that make life interesting.
